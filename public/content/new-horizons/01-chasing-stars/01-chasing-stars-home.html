New Horizons Part 1: Chasing Stars

“To create is to live twice.”

– Albert Camus

Markus Bowland is a dipshit fail son. Wealthy and talented, he squandered every opportunity anyone could ever hope for. Emotionally crippled at a young age by a tragic loss, the expectations of him left him unwilling to try, unwilling to put himself in a position to fail in any way. Working a dead end job, keeping his wealth secret from his peers and mooching off of his sucessful brother when necessary, afraid event to dare a relationship with a human woman in favour of his favourite simulant prostitute.

Largely just to annoy his brother he enters a lottery to win a spot on the New Horizon generational starship. When when he wins the spot and learns more about the mission and the people behind it, he finds himself strangely unable to sell the seat and not only considers going himself, but daring to seek out meaning in his life for the first time, and ask why he's resisted it so hard for so long.
