I've always been a big techie; I've always been interested in hardware and how things work. I've always enjoyed using cool technology, as well as curiuos about what goes on under the hood, and what makes it all go. Going to BCIT for software development was a bit of a gamble, but it's paying off. I think I've taken to it very well though. I really enjoy the work and am find the content compelling. I'm so eager to hit the ground and get to work!

More personally, I'm a lifelong learner and am always trying to learn everything about everything. I have an 800 hour backlog podcast playlist, I enjoy running and would like to run a marathon some day. I enjoy video games; I'm a Nintendo boy for life but I also love my PS4 now. I love science fiction and comedy, especially put together! Good compelling drama is wonderful as well of course.
